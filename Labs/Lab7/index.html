Biography

Hey everyone, my name is Manjana Sadhu and I am a UPS truck driver. I have travelled across all the land, from beautiful New Jersey to (stinky) California. I have every route and map memorized from my 2 years of driving. In need of a rest stop? Just send me your exact coordinates and I can direct you to the nearest one from memory.

Throughout my experience as a truck driver, I have seen the craziest things you'll ever see. From zombie invasions on the interstate to seeing a baby giraffe be born, I've seen it all. I always have a good story to tell, so I'm a great dinner party guest.
